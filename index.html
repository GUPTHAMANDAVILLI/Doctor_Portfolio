Services

Comprehensive Cardiac Care

From preventive screenings to advanced interventions, I offer a full spectrum of cardiovascular services.

🩹

Preventive Cardiology

Risk assessment, cholesterol management, and lifestyle counseling to keep your heart healthy for life.

💓

Echocardiography

Non-invasive ultrasound imaging to evaluate heart structure, function, and blood flow in real-time.

🏃

Stress Testing

Exercise and pharmacologic stress tests to assess how your heart performs under physical exertion.

🩸

Interventional Procedures

Cardiac catheterization, angioplasty, and stent placement with cutting-edge minimally invasive techniques.

Arrhythmia Management

Diagnosis and treatment of irregular heart rhythms, including Holter monitoring and ablation procedures.

Heart Failure Care

Comprehensive management of heart failure with advanced therapies and coordinated multidisciplinary care.
